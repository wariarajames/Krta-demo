What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where users are tricked into entering sensitive information into fake websites that look real.

Warning Signs

  • Suspicious or unfamiliar URLs
  • Requests for passwords or PINs
  • Urgent or threatening messages
  • Poor spelling or design inconsistencies

Protection Tips

  • Always verify website links
  • Use two-factor authentication
  • Never share credentials via unknown links
  • Check HTTPS and domain carefully
